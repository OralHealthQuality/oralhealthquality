The **Texas Center for Oral Healthcare Quality and Safety** at UTHealth Houston conducts research and builds practical tools that make dental care safer, higher-quality, and more measurable. Our work spans diagnostic safety, patient safety, quality measurement, large-scale dental data, patient-reported outcomes, antimicrobial stewardship, and AI-driven diagnostics — and everything we produce is meant to be used by clinicians, researchers, educators, and policymakers.
