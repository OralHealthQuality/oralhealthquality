The Center has been awarded a new R01 from the National Institute of Dental and Craniofacial Research — *PREDICT: Advancing Periodontitis Care with AI-Driven Diagnostics and Clinical Decision Support* (R01 DE035894). The multiple-PI project, led with Drs. Xiaoqian Jiang, Chun-Teh Lee, and Shayan Shams, will build and validate artificial-intelligence tools that interpret dental radiographs and EHR data to improve the diagnosis and management of periodontitis.

See the [PREDICT project page](/projects/predict-periodontitis-ai/) for details.
