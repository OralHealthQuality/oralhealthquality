## Goal

BigMouth is a dental data repository that aggregates de-identified clinical data from the electronic health records of multiple dental schools and care organizations. Its purpose is to give researchers, educators, and quality teams a large, standardized dataset to study oral health, care patterns, and outcomes at a scale no single institution could achieve.

## Approach

Originating from NLM-funded work to build an inter-university oral health research database (G08 LM010075), BigMouth standardizes data from contributing institutions into a common model, applies rigorous de-identification and governance, and makes curated datasets available to approved investigators. The repository serves as the data backbone for quality measurement, patient safety, diagnostics, and pain-outcomes research.

## Impact

BigMouth has grown into one of the largest dental EHR research resources in the United States, supporting dozens of studies and enabling a true learning health system for dentistry — where routine clinical data continuously informs better care.
