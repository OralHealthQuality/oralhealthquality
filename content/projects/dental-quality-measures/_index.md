## Goal

You cannot improve what you do not measure. This project develops electronic clinical quality measures for dentistry — automated, EHR-based metrics of whether recommended care was delivered — and tests whether putting those measures in front of clinicians actually improves care.

## Approach

Through *Implementing Dental Quality Measures in Practice* (R01 DE024166, multiple-PI with Dr. Elsbeth Kalenderian), the team specifies dental eMeasures, validates them against chart review, and implements them in real dental practices with audit-and-feedback dashboards. Measures span areas such as sealant placement, caries prevention, tobacco screening and cessation, and safety.

## Impact

The resulting measures and the lessons from implementing them inform national quality programs, EHR vendors, and dental organizations moving toward value-based and accountable oral health care.
