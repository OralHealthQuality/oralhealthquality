## Goal

Periodontitis is common, consequential, and frequently under-diagnosed. PREDICT develops and validates artificial-intelligence tools that read dental radiographs and electronic health record data to detect periodontal bone loss, support accurate diagnosis and staging, and guide treatment decisions.

## Approach

*PREDICT: Advancing Periodontitis Care with AI-Driven Diagnostics and Clinical Decision Support* (R01 DE035894, multiple-PI with Drs. Xiaoqian Jiang, Chun-Teh Lee, and Shayan Shams) builds deep-learning models for radiographic bone-loss detection, develops rule-based and machine-learning algorithms to identify periodontal diagnoses from EHR data, and integrates these into clinical decision support that fits real workflows.

## Impact

By bringing validated AI diagnostics to the chairside, PREDICT aims to reduce missed and delayed periodontitis diagnoses and to demonstrate a responsible model for AI-driven decision support in dentistry.
