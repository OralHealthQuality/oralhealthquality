Dr. Muhammad F. Walji (MS, PhD, FACMI) directs the Texas Center for Oral Healthcare Quality and Safety at UTHealth Houston, with faculty appointments at both the McWilliams School of Biomedical Informatics and the School of Dentistry. His research applies biomedical informatics to improve the quality and safety of dental care — including standardized diagnostic terminology, electronic clinical quality measures, dental patient safety, large-scale dental data through the BigMouth repository, and the responsible use of artificial intelligence in dentistry. He is a Fellow of the American College of Medical Informatics.
